Daily Fantasy Values

Daily Fantasy Values is a site built to make it easy to see which daily players have the best value when compared to their salary. Each player is broken down into their average points vs salary, cost vs average salary, and their standard deviation of points vs other players in their categories.

This site gets updated every week with the new salaries and should hopefully provide better insight into who you should pick for your roster.
